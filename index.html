ABOUT US

Thousands of UMD party attendees, fraternity brothers and sorority sisters leave parties every weekend. Many times, people feel unsafe walking back either to the bus stops or to their dorms. Safety can be an issue on college campuses, especially at night when someone must walk home alone.

 

Strength in Numbers not only ensures the safety of the individuals walking back home, but would also enlist fraternities, ridding them of the stigma that they do not make sure everyone gets home safely.

Our service will include members of fraternities walking party attendees to specified locations throughout the outskirts of the College Park campus. At these points, SN volunteers will walk these students home, leaving every 15 minutes.

HOW WE WORK

Our organization will lower criminal activity and enhance the sense of safety felt by all members of the College Park community.
